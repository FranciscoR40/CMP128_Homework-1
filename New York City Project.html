New York City

New York City is what we call the City that never sleeps. It's very active and has lots of amazing places that someone can go to if they ever decide to visit. It is very diverse when it comes to the culture and offers things to do for everyone. NYC is located at the southern tip of the State of New York, the city is the center of the New York metropolitan area.

Weather

When it comes to the weather in NYC the seasons come in strong. The summers are very hot, warm, and humid, the winters are very cold and windy, throughout the year it can get rainy and cloudy.
